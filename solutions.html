Best Fit Signals

When teams usually start looking.

Role-Based Fit

Built around the responsibilities different teams carry.

Each team works the same receivables book, but the operating pressure is different. Ledgewave supports the three buyer and user lenses that come up most often.

For AR teams

Built for AR managers, collectors, shared-service analysts, and operators who need a better daily queue and a reliable follow-up record.

  • Prioritize accounts by exposure, aging, ownership, and next action
  • Keep notes, promise dates, sent history, and invoice context in one place
  • Make dunning execution consistent without trapping collectors in rigid scripts

For finance leaders

Built for controllers, CFOs, finance directors, and FP&A partners who need a clearer cash story backed by operational detail.

  • Connect expected cash to receivables status, planned billing, and collector activity
  • Explain forecast movement with account-level context instead of manual recap tabs
  • Review exposure, risk, and follow-up progress before leadership meetings

For multi-entity ops

Built for finance operations teams running receivables across multiple entities, ERPs, currencies, books, or shared-service groups.

  • Normalize ERP exports, accounting data, secure files, and CSV imports into one operating model
  • Segment queues by entity, owner, priority, status, or collection motion
  • Give leaders a rollup without forcing every team into the same local process

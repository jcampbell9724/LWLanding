Frequently asked questions

Do we need clean ERP data before starting?

No. Many teams begin with exports or imperfect accounting data and improve workflow structure over time.

Will this replace our ERP?

No. Ledgewave works alongside your ERP and existing finance workflows.

Can I change plans later?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade as your operational needs change.

Is there a contract?

Monthly self-serve subscriptions renew monthly. Annual self-serve subscriptions are billed for a one-year term and renew annually. Enterprise terms are handled through an order form.

What happens if we exceed our invoice volume?

Small overages are fine. Sustained growth typically triggers a plan upgrade conversation.

How long does setup take?

Core plans can launch in under a day using exports or accounting data. Larger ERP and multi-entity deployments typically take 2-6 weeks depending on workflow complexity.

Is our data secure?

Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Enterprise plans support advanced security and access controls.

Do you offer annual pricing discounts?

Yes. Annual self-serve subscriptions are billed in advance for a one-year term and include a 10% discount.

Can we cancel anytime?

Monthly self-serve subscriptions may be canceled effective at the end of the current monthly billing period. Annual subscriptions may be canceled before renewal and remain active through the paid annual term; annual payments are non-refundable.

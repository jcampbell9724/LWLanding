Receivables Workflow FAQ

Questions teams ask before they move into a real evaluation.

What is Ledgewave?

Ledgewave is receivables workflow and cash forecasting software for finance teams. It connects receivables imports, planned billing, prioritization, draft follow-up, invoice history, and expected-cash review in one system.

Who is it built for?

Collections leaders, controllers, AR managers, shared-services teams, and finance operations groups that have outgrown spreadsheet-heavy receivables workflow.

Does it replace our ERP?

No. Ledgewave sits on the operating layer around receivables execution and forecast review. Teams can start with exported files instead of replacing the system of record.

Can teams start before every data path is live?

Yes. Many teams begin with receivables and planned billing files, then add customer-specific API ingest later if the workflow and field mapping require it.
